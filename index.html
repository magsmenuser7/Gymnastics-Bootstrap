The Gymnastics Association of Andhra Pradesh (GAAP) is a governing body for gymnastics in the state of Andhra Pradesh. GAAP is responsible for promoting the sport at various levels, from grassroots development to elite competitions.


The association typically organizes state-level championships, training programs, and workshops for gymnasts and coaches to enhance their skills and prepare them for national and international competitions.


  • Hosting workshops with national and international coaches.

  • Organizing talent identification camps across various districts.

  • Offering athletes opportunities to compete at the national level.

GAAP organizes competitions, training camps, and workshops to enhance the skills of gymnasts and coaches. The association also plays a key role in representing the state in national-level gymnastics competitions, helping athletes gain exposure and improve their performances.
